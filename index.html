SakhiDhan

Empowering Women Through
Smart & Secure Finance

A blockchain-based microloan and savings platform for Self-Help Groups (SHGs).
Save, borrow, and grow together with trust and transparency.

Loans

The system provides accessible and transparent financing options. Users can apply for loans with flexible repayment plans tailored to their needs.

Savings

The feature enables users to securely store and grow their funds. It encourages financial stability through smart, blockchain-backed savings plans.

Repayments

Borrowers benefit from automated schedules, rewards for timely payments, and penalties for delays, ensuring financial discipline.
